# Getting Started with Agentics

Agentics Foundation is a global community building open-source agentic AI systems. This tutorial gets a new member from zero to first contribution.

## 1. What is Agentics?

A nonprofit foundation focused on making agentic AI accessible. The community runs around three pillars:

- **Open-source projects** — frameworks, MCP servers, and tooling
- **Education** — tutorials, talks, and hands-on workshops
- **Events** — meetups, hackathons, and partner sessions

Site: https://agentics.org · Members: 100K+

## 2. Join the community

1. Visit https://agentics.org and pick a membership tier
2. Sign up — confirms email and adds you to the community Slack/Discord
3. Introduce yourself in the #intros channel

## 3. Your first 30 minutes

- Read the home page and `/about` (use the `agentics-org` plugin: `/agentics fetch /`)
- Browse upcoming events (use the `agentics-events` plugin: `/events list`)
- Pick a topic from the project list (use `/agentics fetch /partners`)

## 4. Next steps

- **Build your first agent**: see the `build-agent` skill
- **Integrate MCP**: see the `mcp-integration` skill
- **Find an event**: `/events list`

## Output style when teaching

When walking a learner through this:
- One step at a time, ask before moving on
- Cite agentics.org URLs for ground truth
- Encourage hands-on: "open this page, tell me what you see"

# Agentics Onboarding Flow

A 4-step path from "interested" to "shipping."

## Step 1 — Sign up

- Visit https://agentics.org
- Choose a membership tier on `/membership`
- Verify email; you'll be added to the community channels

Confirm with the learner: "Did you receive the welcome email? What channel was the invite for?"

## Step 2 — Introduce yourself

- Post in `#intros` — name, what brought you here, one thing you want to build
- React to two other intros — community is reciprocal

## Step 3 — Pick a starter project

Three good first contributions:
1. **Try a tutorial end-to-end** (use the `agentics-tutorials` plugin)
2. **Build a small MCP plugin** that exposes one tool you'd actually use
3. **Show up to an event** (use the `agentics-events` plugin: `/events list`)

Ask the learner which path matches their goals.

## Step 4 — Register for the next event

- `/events list` to see what's coming up
- Pick one in the next 14 days; register on Luma
- Add to calendar; show up

## Anti-patterns to flag gently

- Lurking for months without engaging — the community is built around interaction
- Trying to consume "all the docs" before doing anything — start small, learn by shipping
- Building in isolation — share early in `#showcase`

## Output style

- One step per turn; wait for confirmation before advancing
- Celebrate completion of each step
- If they get stuck, shrink the next step (don't give up — give a smaller next move)
